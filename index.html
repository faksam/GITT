Achievements

Success

We succeeded in acquiring and providing very large Internet bandwidth with phenomenal efficiency to our clients. Thus we established a Wide Area Network (WAN) connecting West African region such as presidential places Ouagadougou in Burkina-Faso and Bamako in Mali, Abuja and Kano in addition to Local Government Headquarters to the State Capital in Jigawa State, Abuja and Kano. Metropolitan Area Networks (MAN) were also established linking various state institutions to the network via point to multi point connections.

Awards

We won an International Award "The Wireless Communities Best Practices" on Digital Wireless Inclusion (DWI) in 2005, from The World Society of Information Systems (WSIS).

We are recognized as pacesetters that has developed a robust network predicated on world-class standards which explains why it was considered to be the major contributor to the Federal Governments Galaxy Backbone project.

Pacesetters

We are recognized capacity builder in the ICT industry in Nigeria, it has trained a number of staff in various fields that are currently considered to be among the best crop of IT professionals, working in various national and multinational organizations such as Microsoft, Central Bank of Nigeria, Galaxy Backbone Plc., Nigerian Internet Exchange Point (NIXP), Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation.

Reputation

The company is a recognized market leader that has developed a robust network predicated on world-class standards which explains why it was considered to be the major contributor to the Federal Governments Galaxy Backbone project.

Alliance

In 2005, We became the official partner of Mobile Telecommunications Limited (M-Tel) in which it successfully carried m-tel traffic using E1 clear channel capacity via satellite in Dutse, Hadejia, Gumel, Babura in Jigawa State, and Geidam in Yobe State.

Bridging Digital Divide

In 2004 We became a full-fledged Internet Service Provider (ISP) in Abuja servicing Federal MDA’s and other private sector organizations.
